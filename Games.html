BORE DOME (2022)

Experimental game developed with Aggelos Efstathopoulos (birgif), and produced by William Wagner, Digital Moment Award at A-MAZE 2022, and Best Student Game at IGF 2020

BORE DOME is exhibited on the Black Diamond in Copenhagen as parts of the #dkgame exhibition (until January 2024).

The roles in the project were pretty much shared between Aggelos and me, and we took part in almost all the work that each other produced. High level concepting and ideation was shared (Game Direction), and Art Direction was initially set by Aggelos, and adapted and reinterpreted by me. In a more practical / technical sense, my work included:

  • Most 3D assets (characters and environments) and materials/shaders
  • Most 3D animations
  • Most in game FX
  • Engine tools
  • Game Design
  • Concept Art / Marketing Art
  • Music and audio effects

Traces (2018)

Mobile game made with DADIU, featuring exploration on a semi open-world, focused on diverse player movement, using your shield to slide, surf and glide.

My role in this project was Art Director. I coordinated two artists with a team of programmers, the game director, project manager, and design team. I also ensured the vision for the game was met while keeping the mobile limitations in check. Other work included:

  • Concept Art
  • Marketing Art
  • Character Design
