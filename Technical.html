The Game of Video Game Objects

During a couple of months I helped academic Jesper Juul creating a level for his interactive research piece. I created assets, remixed some other already there, adjusted lighting areas, FX, and designed a path for the player to encounter the different texts/experiences.

The experience and paper can be found at Jesper's website.

IOs build for Augment-X

Building Esben's app through x-code, and handling the appstore needs for publishing. Debugging on X-code wasn't fun, but it worked out.

VR Exhibition

Setting up a collaborative virtual stage for students to design and incorporate assets on a common scene, and building it for Oculus Quest 2. The main challenge was to handle git for a large amount of inexperienced users, introduce them to workflows, and assure performance.

The exhibition took place at Det Kongelige Akademi in May 2023.

Christmas VR app for kids

Visual research, character design, animation work and technical help with Unity for Timestory VR Aps in 2018-19. The experience was supposed to be a short (5 mins) immersive situation where kids help Santa by saying some magic words.
