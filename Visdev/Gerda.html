Gerda: A Flame in Winter
Overpaints

Overpaints made for the game "Gerda: A Flame in Winter" during summer 2022. Screenshots were given while still developing and tweaking lighting and visuals, so the work consisted mostly on repainting light, adding characters, and effects, while keeping the painterly artstyle of the game.

Process

The work was done on top of screenshots provided with not final graphics, to which characters and lighting was added.
